How it works

      
Paste CSV or drop a file. The tool detects the delimiter (comma, semicolon, tab or pipe), handles quoted fields (with escapes like ""), and converts to JSON in your browser. Choose headers on/off, trim, cast numbers, and pick array-of-objects or array-of-arrays output. Nothing is uploaded.


      
FAQs

      

        Is this free and private?
        
Yes. It runs entirely on your device — no servers, no uploads.

      

      

        How are numbers handled?
        
Enable “Preserve number types” to auto-cast integers/floats. Turn it off to keep values as strings.
